# Editorial Design Research

Reference screenshots are stored locally in `research/screenshots/` and are ignored from Git because they are third-party captures.

## Useful Patterns

- The Verge: strong split between curated lead package and chronological feed.
- TechCrunch: bold section labels, compact newsletter modules, clear category rails.
- Semafor: publication-like masthead, topical navigation, strong article typography.
- Rest of World: dense latest rail, center lead story, right-side secondary package.
- Platformer: focused newsletter/product positioning with a clean latest feed.

## Applied To This Site

- Lead story plus market brief rail on the homepage.
- Category/channel navigation above story grids.
- Compact article cards with consistent badges and source metadata.
- Article pages with featured-image metadata, video cross-links, source count, and related stories.
