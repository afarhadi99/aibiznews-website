Microsoft Azure is rolling out NVIDIA GB200 NVL72 systems to accelerate both foundation model training and production inference. These racks link Blackwell-generation GPUs over NVLink Switch, paired with Azure's orchestration and storage services for turnkey deployment.

## Why it matters

- Faster time to train and deploy large models
- Higher throughput for multimodal and agentic workloads
- Integrated with Kubernetes, ONNX Runtime, DeepSpeed, and Azure AI

## What to watch

- Queue times and capacity by region
- Pricing and job scheduling policies
- Reference architectures for fine-tuning and RAG at scale
