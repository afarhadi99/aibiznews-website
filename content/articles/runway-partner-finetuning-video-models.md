## Introduction

 Runway announced that select partners can fine-tune its video generation models, unlocking industry-specific styles and workflows. The program targets studios, brands, and creative toolmakers who need custom aesthetics and tighter control over motion and composition.

 Fine-tuning expands beyond prompt engineering: teams can train on proprietary datasets to match brand guidelines, cinematic language, or product catalog visuals. Expect improved temporal consistency, better subject fidelity, and enterprise features like usage analytics and content provenance.
