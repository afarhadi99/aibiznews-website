## Introduction

 Amazon announced the general availability of Bedrock AgentCore, a managed framework for building production agents with tool use, memory, secure data access, and step-level orchestration. AgentCore standardizes how developers define tools, schemas, and guardrails, with tracing that plugs into CloudWatch and AWS X-Ray.

 GA adds tighter policy controls, evaluation harnesses, and integrations across Bedrock models, Knowledge Bases, and enterprise connectors. Teams can deploy agents inside VPCs, enforce IAM-based permissions, and log decisions for audit - critical for regulated workloads.

 Expect adoption in customer support, IT automation, and data workflows where deterministic tool calls and observability matter. AgentCore positions Bedrock as a full-stack platform for governed agentic apps at scale.
