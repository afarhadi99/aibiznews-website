## Introduction

Anthropic expanded in Japan with a new Tokyo office and an MoC with the Japan AI Safety Institute to advance AI evaluation and governance.

Anthropic officially opened a Tokyo office and signed a Memorandum of Cooperation with Japan's AI Safety Institute. The partnership focuses on evaluation methods and monitoring emerging risks, alongside growing commercial work in the region. Strategically, it's a template for aligning frontier model capabilities with national safety standards. Expect localized product pushes-Japanese language strength, enterprise connectors, and compliance features-plus research ties into benchmarks and red-teaming. With AWS re:Invent on deck, financial-services and government pilots are likely first in line. Bottom line: safety, policy, and product are converging in-market, not just in white papers.
