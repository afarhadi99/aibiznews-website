## Introduction

NVIDIA and Oracle outlined plans to build DOE's largest AI supercomputer, bringing modern AI tooling to national-scale scientific workloads.

Oracle and NVIDIA announced a collaboration to build the U.S. Department of Energy's largest AI supercomputer. The system will accelerate scientific discovery across physics, climate, and bio domains with next-gen GPUs and high-bandwidth fabrics. Beyond raw FLOPs, the plan integrates NVIDIA's software stack for simulation, data processing, and generative models-aiming to shorten the loop from experiment to insight. If procurement and delivery stay on track, researchers could see orders-of-magnitude gains for training foundation models on scientific data. It's another signal that national labs are standardizing on AI supercomputing as a core instrument-not just for language, but for the sciences at large.
