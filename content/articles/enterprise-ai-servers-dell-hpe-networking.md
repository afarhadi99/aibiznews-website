Enterprises are rolling out GPU-dense servers from Dell and HPE, while standardizing networks at 100-400G to support training, retrieval, and large-scale inference.

## What's new

- Validated blueprints for AI training and RAG
- Thermal and power designs for high-density racks
- Spine-leaf networks with QoS and optical upgrades
- Integrated observability to maintain throughput

## Why it matters

AI performance is a system outcome. Balanced servers and high-speed switching reduce bottlenecks and make scaling predictable.
