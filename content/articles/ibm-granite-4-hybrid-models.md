## Introduction

 IBM's Granite 4.0 introduces a hybrid model family combining Transformer and state-space (e.g., Mamba-style) components to improve efficiency and long-context performance. Packaged for enterprise use, Granite 4.0 lands alongside ISO/IEC 42001 AI management certification and updated governance tooling.

 The pitch: lower cost per token, better throughput on commodity hardware, and strong privacy controls with on-prem or VPC deployment. IBM pairs the models with compliance assets, red-team reports, and policy templates to help regulated industries move faster without sacrificing oversight.

 Expect integration across watsonx, mainframes, and hybrid cloud, with a focus on retrieval-augmented workflows and agentic automation within enterprise boundaries.
