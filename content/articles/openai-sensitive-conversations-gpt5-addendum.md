## Introduction

 OpenAI published updates on handling sensitive conversations in ChatGPT and released an addendum to the GPT-5 system card focused on risk evaluation in high-impact domains. The changes detail stricter response patterns, opt-in disclosures, and clearer refusal behaviors for topics that intersect with health, finance, politics, and personal data.

 The GPT-5 addendum outlines evaluation protocols, red-team scenarios, and mitigations for jailbreaks and prompt injection. It emphasizes layered defenses: policy tuning, model-based classifiers, and post-deployment monitoring that feeds back into rapid mitigation.

 Bottom line: more predictable refusals, better provenance of safety decisions, and expanded documentation. Enterprises should map these behaviors to internal compliance and update agent policies to account for stricter boundaries.
