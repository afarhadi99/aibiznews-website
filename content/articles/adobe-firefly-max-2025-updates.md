Adobe announced Firefly Image 5, generative audio/speech, and Firefly Foundry for on-brand models.

## What's included

- Layered image editing and 4MP native outputs
- Generate Soundtrack and Generate Speech
- Firefly Foundry for proprietary models and governance
- Conversational assistant preview across apps

## Why it matters

Teams can move ideas to deliverables faster while maintaining brand control and auditability.
