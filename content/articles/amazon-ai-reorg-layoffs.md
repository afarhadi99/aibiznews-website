Amazon is consolidating teams and resources to focus on core AI initiatives-model development, AWS Bedrock and Q, agentic workflows, and AI-assisted retail and logistics. Alongside this shift, the company plans to reduce approximately 14,000 corporate roles.

## What's changing

- Centralization around AI programs in AWS and retail
- Expanded investment in generative features for storefronts, sellers, and advertisers
- Continued robotics and computer vision deployment in fulfillment
- Tighter linkage between AWS silicon, models, and enterprise adoption

## Why it matters

Amazon is repositioning to accelerate AI-driven product cycles and operational efficiency. Short-term disruption aside, the strategy signals faster delivery of AI capabilities across the entire Amazon ecosystem.
