## Introduction

Salesforce's Dreamforce 2025 in October delivered a bold vision for enterprise AI: the Agentic Enterprise, where autonomous AI agents reason, act, and collaborate alongside human workers. Agentforce 360 represents Salesforce's comprehensive platform for building, deploying, and governing enterprise-grade AI agents across the entire business ecosystem.

At Dreamforce 2025 in October, Salesforce unveiled Agentforce 360 and declared the rise of the Agentic Enterprise. Not AI tools that assist humans. Autonomous agents that reason, act, and collaborate alongside people. This is Salesforce's biggest bet on the future of work.

Agentforce 360 spans the entire Salesforce ecosystem. Platform, data, customer apps, and Slack integration. The vision: AI agents that don't just answer questions but actually get work done. Sales agents that qualify leads, draft proposals, and update CRM automatically. IT agents that troubleshoot issues and provision access. HR agents that onboard employees and answer policy questions.

The key innovation is Intelligent Context. Most AI agents only understand structured data in databases. Agentforce pulls information from everything: PDFs, images, flowcharts, email threads, Slack conversations. It understands the messy, unstructured reality of how businesses actually operate.

Salesforce announced deeper Slack integration in October 2025. Agentforce Sales, IT, and HR surface directly in Slack threads. The new Slackbot is a personalized AI agent that learns about you, offers insights proactively, and connects information across your entire workspace. Gmail, Outlook, and Dropbox connectors launch early 2026.

This positions Salesforce to orchestrate first and third-party AI agents across the enterprise. Not just Salesforce's agents. Anyone's agents, managed through a unified platform with proper governance, security, and compliance. That's the play.

The enterprise AI competition is heating up. Microsoft, Google, and independent platforms like UiPath are all racing to become the operating system for AI agents. Salesforce is leveraging two decades of customer data integration and trust to claim that crown.
