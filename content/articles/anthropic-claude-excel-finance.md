Anthropic introduced an Excel add-in and finance connectors so analysts can work with Claude directly in spreadsheets.

## What's included

- Excel sidebar (research preview) with read/write and cell-level citations
- Connectors for live market data and earnings transcripts
- Agent skills for DCFs and coverage workflows
- Audit logs for review and compliance

## Why it matters

Finance teams can speed analysis while preserving transparency and control. Spreadsheet-native AI is becoming a competitive battleground.
