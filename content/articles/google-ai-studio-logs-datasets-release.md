## Introduction

Google rolled out new logs and datasets in AI Studio to streamline debugging, evaluation, and dataset management for AI apps and agents.

Google AI Studio introduced logs and datasets features to speed up agent and app iteration. Builders can now capture runs, inspect tool calls, compare prompts, and curate datasets for targeted improvement-without duct-taping third-party analytics. It's a quality-of-life update that matters: faster debugging loops, reproducible experiments, and cleaner handoff from prototype to production. Paired with Model Context Protocol and Vertex integrations, teams get a clearer path to govern prompts, data, and evaluations end-to-end. Expect this to be table-stakes across platforms as agent workflows get more complex and compliance demands rise.
