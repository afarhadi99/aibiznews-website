Oracle introduced the OCI Zettascale10 computing cluster, accelerated by NVIDIA GPUs and networking, to support high-performance AI training and inference at scale. The design emphasizes consistent performance, capacity planning, and ease of deployment for enterprises moving from pilots to production.

## Key updates

- Multi-gigawatt-class capacity design for large AI clusters.
- NVIDIA AI Enterprise and NIM microservices available natively in OCI for streamlined inference, RAG, and agentic apps.
- Oracle AI Data Platform adds GPU-accelerated analytics via RAPIDS for Apache Spark.

## Why it matters

The stack reduces integration overhead. Teams can provision compute, models, and data tooling in one environment with governance and security controls.

## Use cases

- RAG search across enterprise documents
- ETL + training pipelines on unified infrastructure
- Scalable inference with model microservices
