NVIDIA and US manufacturing leaders are aligning around "physical AI" - deploying robotics and computer vision with factory-scale digital twins built in Omniverse.

## What's included

- Omniverse blueprints for factory-scale digital twins
- Reference stacks for inspection, assembly, and logistics
- NIM microservices for perception and inference
- High-speed networking and NVLink for deterministic performance

## Why it matters

Manufacturers can move faster from simulation to production with validated workflows and partner support. The focus is on measurable wins: higher yield, fewer stoppages, and shorter time to ramp.
